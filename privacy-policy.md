# Joy Quotes – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

Joy Quotes (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Joy Quotes mobile application.

By using Joy Quotes, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
Joy Quotes uses **Sign in with Apple** for authentication.

We may receive:
- Apple-provided user identifier
- Display name (if granted)

We do NOT collect your Apple password.

---

### 1.2 User Generated Content
When using the app, you may create:
- Quotes
- Comments and replies
- Stories (photo/video)
- Voice messages
- Private messages
- Profile information (username, display name, profile photo)

This content is stored securely using Apple CloudKit infrastructure.

---

### 1.3 Location Data (Optional)
If you grant permission, we may use approximate location data to help you discover nearby users.

Location is:
- Used only while the app is active
- Not sold to third parties
- Not used for precise tracking

You may disable location access at any time in device settings.

---

### 1.4 Notifications
We use **OneSignal** to deliver push notifications for:
- Likes
- Comments
- Mentions
- Follows
- Messages
- Story interactions

Push tokens are used solely to deliver notifications.

---

### 1.5 Advertising & Tracking
Joy Quotes displays ads via **Google AdMob**.

If you grant tracking permission (ATT):
- Advertising identifiers may be used to personalize ads.

If you deny tracking:
- Ads will be non-personalized.

We do not sell your personal data.

---

### 1.6 Subscription & Purchases
VIP subscriptions are processed securely via Apple StoreKit.
We do not store your payment information.

Apple manages:
- Billing
- Renewals
- Payment processing

---

### 1.7 Music Access (Optional)
If you add music to stories, we may request permission to access Apple Music.
This is only used to allow music selection within stories.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide core app functionality
- Display user-generated content
- Enable messaging and interactions
- Deliver notifications
- Improve performance and stability
- Prevent abuse and enforce community rules

---

## 3. Content Moderation

Joy Quotes allows user-generated content.

Users can:
- Report inappropriate content
- Block other users

We reserve the right to remove content that violates community guidelines.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored using:
- Apple CloudKit infrastructure
- Secure Apple services

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active.

If you delete your account:
- Personal profile data is deleted
- Associated content may be anonymized where necessary for community integrity

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Joy Quotes is not intended for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You may:
- Delete your account
- Request data removal
- Disable permissions (camera, location, microphone, tracking) in device settings

---

## 8. Third-Party Services

We use:

- Apple CloudKit (data storage)
- OneSignal (push notifications)
- Google AdMob (advertising)
- Apple StoreKit (subscriptions)
- Apple MusicKit (music integration)

Each service operates under its own privacy policy.
